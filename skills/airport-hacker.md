## Lounge Access

### Priority Pass
Priority Pass is the largest independent lounge network with 1,400+ lounges in 600+ airports worldwide.

**Cards that include Priority Pass unlimited visits:**
- Chase Sapphire Reserve ($550/year) — you + guests free
- Amex Platinum ($695/year) — you only, guests $50 each
- Capital One Venture X ($395/year) — you + 2 guests free (best value)

**How to use:** Download Priority Pass app, show card or app + boarding pass at lounge entrance.

### Centurion Lounges (Amex Platinum)
Best domestic lounges in the US — free food, open bar, spa services at some locations. Available at 20+ major US airports.

### Airline Lounges
- American Admirals Club: free with Citi AAdvantage Executive card or business class ticket
- United Club: free with Chase United Club Infinite card or international business class
- Delta Sky Club: free with Amex Delta Reserve card or business class ticket

## TSA PreCheck and Global Entry — Get It Free

**Always get Global Entry ($100) not just PreCheck ($78)** — Global Entry includes PreCheck plus fast customs re-entry from international travel.

**Cards that reimburse the fee:**
- Amex Platinum, Chase Sapphire Reserve, Capital One Venture X, Citi Prestige

**How:** Apply at cbp.gov, pay fee, card reimburses automatically.

## CLEAR ($189/year)
Skips biometric ID check entirely. Free with Amex Platinum ($189 annual credit) or Delta Diamond status. Worth it for frequent travelers at major airports.

## Pro Airport Moves
- Book lounges in advance via LoungeBuddy app for day passes ($30-50)
- Long layovers: Singapore, Dubai, Tokyo, Doha have excellent airside transit hotels
- Some airlines offer free layover city tours: Icelandair, Singapore Airlines, Turkish Airlines
- Red-eye flights: window seat, noise canceling headphones, eye mask, avoid alcohol
