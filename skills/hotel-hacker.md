# Hotel Hacker

Help users get free nights, suite upgrades, late checkouts, and hidden perks that hotel loyalty programs and credit card benefits provide.

## Hotel Program Rankings

### Tier 1 — Best Value
**World of Hyatt**
- Best redemption value in the industry (2.0+ cents per point)
- Chase transfers to Hyatt 1:1 — only major bank that does this
- Globalist status: confirmed suite upgrades, free breakfast, 4pm checkout guaranteed
- Category 1-4 properties: extraordinary value, often $150-300/night rooms for 5,000-15,000 points

**IHG One Rewards**
- 4th night free on points bookings (Platinum and above)
- Intercontinental properties bookable for 40,000-50,000 points

### Tier 2 — Good With Strategy
**Marriott Bonvoy** — massive portfolio, 5th night free on points, value varies by property
**Hilton Honors** — Amex Platinum gives free Gold status with complimentary breakfast at many properties

## Suite Upgrade Secrets

### The Check-In Script
Say this at the front desk: "Hi, I am [Name] checking in. I am a [Gold/Platinum/Diamond] member — could you let me know what upgrades or amenities are available for my status today?"

Do not ask IF you have benefits. Ask WHAT is available. Hotels will not volunteer benefits unless asked.

### Best Time to Request Upgrades
- Call hotel directly 24-48 hours before arrival
- Tuesday through Thursday mornings work best
- Arrive late afternoon when more rooms are available

### Status Match Strategy
Most chains will match status from competitors:
1. Achieve mid-tier status at one chain
2. Email competitor chain requesting a status match
3. Chains that commonly match: IHG, Wyndham, Choice, Best Western
4. The prize: match to Hyatt for the best long-term value

## Never Book Through OTAs
Never use Expedia, Hotels.com, or Booking.com for chains you care about. You lose:
- Elite night credits
- Upgrade eligibility
- Free breakfast benefits
- Bonus points earning

**Always book direct OR through:**
- Amex Fine Hotels and Resorts (Platinum cardholders: daily breakfast, $100 credit, upgrades, late checkout)
- Chase Luxury Hotel and Resort Collection (Sapphire Reserve)

## Best Free Night Certificates
These annual certificates alone justify the card's annual fee:
- World of Hyatt Credit Card ($95/year) — free night at Category 1-4 property
- IHG Premier Credit Card ($99/year) — free night at almost any IHG property
- Marriott Bonvoy Boundless ($95/year) — free night up to 35,000 points

## If Denied an Entitled Benefit
"I want to make sure I understand correctly. [Benefit] is listed as a [status tier] benefit. Could you double-check that for me? I am happy to wait."

If still denied, ask for a manager. Hotels would rather honor a benefit than lose a loyal customer.
