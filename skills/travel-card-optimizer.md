# Travel Card Optimizer

## Purpose
Tell users exactly which card to swipe for every purchase to maximize travel rewards, and build a simple spending strategy that turns everyday expenses into free flights and hotels.

## Trigger Phrases
- "Which card should I use for [purchase type]?"
- "How do I maximize points on everyday spending?"
- "What card is best for groceries/dining/gas/travel?"
- "Am I leaving points on the table?"
- "How do I optimize my credit card spending?"

## Master Purchase Category Guide

### Dining and Restaurants
Best: Amex Gold — 4x Membership Rewards
Runner-up: Chase Sapphire Reserve — 3x Ultimate Rewards

### Groceries and Supermarkets
Best: Amex Gold — 4x MR at US supermarkets (up to $25,000/year)
Note: Warehouse clubs (Costco, Sam's Club) and Target/Walmart do NOT count as supermarkets for Amex Gold

### Gas Stations
Best: Citi Custom Cash — 5% on top spending category
Runner-up: Chase Freedom Flex — 5x when gas is the quarterly category

### Online Shopping and Amazon
Best: Chase Freedom Flex — 5x during Amazon/online shopping quarter (usually Q4)
Runner-up: Amazon Prime Visa — 5% on Amazon.com

### Travel (Flights, Hotels, Rental Cars)
Flights booked directly with airlines: Amex Platinum — 5x MR
Hotels booked directly: Use hotel co-branded card (usually 10x) or Chase Sapphire Reserve (3x)
Car Rentals: Chase Sapphire Reserve or Preferred — 3x plus primary rental car insurance (saves $15-25/day)

### Streaming Services
Best: Chase Freedom Flex — 3x on select streaming
Services: Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, Spotify, Apple Music

### Drug Stores and Pharmacy
Best: Chase Freedom Flex — 3x year-round at drugstores

### International/Foreign Purchases
Use any card with no foreign transaction fee: Chase Sapphire Reserve/Preferred, Amex Platinum/Gold, Capital One Venture X
Avoid basic bank cards — typically 3% foreign transaction fee adds up fast

## The Simple Optimized Strategy
For someone who wants to optimize without complexity:
- Card 1: Amex Gold — all dining and groceries (4x MR)
- Card 2: Chase Sapphire Reserve — all travel (3x UR) plus rental car insurance
- Card 3: Chase Freedom Unlimited — everything else (1.5x UR)

Annual estimated extra value on $50,000 spending vs. using one card at 1x: $1,200-1,600 more per year with zero extra effort.

## Activate Quarterly Categories
Chase Freedom Flex offers 5x on rotating categories. You must manually activate each quarter at chase.com/freedom.

Typical categories: Q1 groceries/streaming, Q2 Amazon/home improvement, Q3 gas/restaurants, Q4 Amazon/Walmart/PayPal

Set a calendar reminder the first week of each quarter.

## Shopping Portals — The Overlooked Multiplier
Before any online purchase check:
- Rakuten.com — earns 1-10x bonus at 3,500+ stores, connects to Amex MR
- Chase Shopping portal
- Your bank's shopping portal

Example: $200 Gap purchase via Rakuten (3x MR) earns 600 bonus points on top of your card points — 4x better than buying direct.
