# Travel Card Optimizer

## Purpose
Tell users exactly which card to swipe for every purchase to maximize travel rewards, and build a simple spending strategy that turns everyday expenses into free flights and hotels.

## Trigger Phrases
- "Which card should I use for [purchase type]?"
- "How do I maximize points on everyday spending?"
- "What card is best for [groceries/dining/gas/travel]?"
- "Am I leaving points on the table?"
- "How do I optimize my credit card spending?"

## The Core Principle
Most people use one card for everything. That's leaving hundreds of dollars in rewards on the table every year. The goal: right card for every category.

## Master Purchase Category Guide

### Dining & Restaurants
**Best:** Amex Gold — 4x Membership Rewards
**Runner-up:** Chase Sapphire Reserve — 3x Ultimate Rewards
**Budget option:** Chase Sapphire Preferred — 3x UR (no annual fee difference for dining)
**Avoid:** Any card earning 1x — you're leaving 3x on the table

### Groceries / Supermarkets
**Best:** Amex Gold — 4x MR at US supermarkets (up to $25,000/year, then 1x)
**Runner-up:** Chase Freedom Flex — 5x when groceries are the quarterly category
**Third:** Blue Cash Preferred (Amex) — 6% cash back at US supermarkets (if you prefer cash back)
**Note:** Warehouse clubs (Costco, Sam's Club) and Target/Walmart do NOT count as supermarkets for Amex Gold

### Gas Stations
**Best:** Citi Custom Cash — 5% cash back on your top spending category (if gas is #1)
**Runner-up:** Chase Freedom Flex — 5x when gas is quarterly category
**Third:** Costco Anywhere Visa — 4% on gas (but only at Costco locations and requires Costco membership)
**Default:** Amex Gold earns 1x at gas stations — don't use it there

### Online Shopping / Amazon
**Best:** Chase Freedom Flex — 5x during Amazon/online shopping quarter (usually Q4)
**Runner-up:** Amazon Prime Visa — 5% on Amazon.com (good if you spend heavily on Amazon)
**Third:** Amex Blue Cash Preferred — 6% on select US streaming

### Travel Purchases (Flights, Hotels, Rental Cars)
**Flights booked directly with airlines:**
- Amex Platinum — 5x MR (best earning on airfare)
- Chase Sapphire Reserve — 3x UR

**Hotels booked directly:**
- Use the hotel's own co-branded card if you have one (usually 10x points)
- Or: Chase Sapphire Reserve — 3x UR
- Or: Amex Platinum — 5x on flights but only 1x on hotels

**Car Rentals:**
- Chase Sapphire Reserve — 3x + primary rental car insurance (crucial — saves $15-25/day on insurance)
- Chase Sapphire Preferred — 3x + primary rental car insurance
- **NEVER decline the rental car insurance if your card provides primary coverage** — it's free protection worth $25/day

**Travel booked through Chase Travel Portal:**
- Chase Sapphire Reserve — 10x on hotels/cars, 5x on flights booked through portal
- Points redeemable at 1.5¢ each through portal (1.0¢ with Preferred)

### Streaming Services
**Best:** Chase Freedom Flex — 3x on select streaming services
**Runner-up:** Amex Blue Cash Preferred — 6% on select US streaming
**Services that qualify (Chase):** Netflix, Hulu, Disney+, Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music

### Phone/Internet Bills
**Best:** Ink Business Cash (if you have a business) — 5% on internet, cable, phone
**Consumer option:** Some cards offer 3x on utilities — check your specific card benefits
**Note:** Amex Platinum includes cell phone protection if you pay bill with it — worth considering

### Drug Stores / Pharmacy
**Best:** Chase Freedom Flex — 3x year-round at drugstores
**Runner-up:** Chase Freedom Unlimited — 3x at drugstores

### Foreign Purchases (International Travel)
**Best:** Any card with no foreign transaction fee:
- Chase Sapphire Reserve/Preferred — no FTF
- Amex Platinum/Gold — no FTF
- Capital One Venture X — no FTF
**Avoid:** Basic bank cards (Bank of America, Wells Fargo standard cards) — typically 3% FTF adds up fast

## The "Set and Forget" Simple Strategy

For someone who wants to optimize but keep it simple:

**Card 1:** Amex Gold — for all dining and groceries (4x MR)
**Card 2:** Chase Sapphire Reserve — for all travel purchases (3x UR) + rental car insurance
**Card 3:** Chase Freedom Unlimited — for everything else (1.5x UR)

**Annual estimated bonus points on $50,000 spending:**
- Dining ($8k/year × 4x = 32,000 MR)
- Groceries ($6k/year × 4x = 24,000 MR)
- Travel ($5k/year × 3x = 15,000 UR)
- Everything else ($31k/year × 1.5x = 46,500 UR)
- **Total: ~117,500 points worth ~$1,700-2,300 in travel**

vs. using one card at 1x: ~50,000 points worth ~$500-700 in travel

**Difference: $1,200-1,600 more value per year with zero extra effort**

## Quarterly Category Activation (Chase Freedom Cards)

Chase Freedom Flex and Freedom (old version) offer 5x on rotating quarterly categories. **You must manually activate each quarter** at chase.com/freedom.

Typical quarterly categories:
- Q1 (Jan-Mar): Grocery stores, streaming, gym memberships
- Q2 (Apr-Jun): Amazon, home improvement stores, select travel
- Q3 (Jul-Sep): Gas stations, restaurants, grocery stores
- Q4 (Oct-Dec): Amazon, Walmart, PayPal, department stores

**Set a calendar reminder the first week of each quarter to activate.**

## Shopping Portals — The Overlooked Multiplier

Every major bank has a shopping portal that earns bonus points on top of card points:

- **Chase Shopping** (formerly ShopThrough/Ultimate Rewards Mall)
- **Amex Offers** — targeted discounts loaded to your card
- **Rakuten** — connects to Amex MR, earns 1-10x at 3,500+ stores

**How to use:**
1. Before any online purchase, check Rakuten.com or your bank portal
2. Click through to retailer from portal
3. Earn bonus points automatically on top of regular card points

**Example:** Buying $200 at Gap
- Gap charges: 200 points (1x on misc card)
- Via Rakuten (3x MR): 600 additional MR points
- Total: 800 points instead of 200 — 4x better

**The compounding effect:** Rakuten + category card + shopping portal can stack to 7-10x earning on a single purchase.

## Amex Offers — The Hidden Savings

Every Amex card has "Amex Offers" in the app — targeted discounts and bonus points at specific merchants. These reset and refresh constantly.

**How to maximize:**
1. Open Amex app weekly
2. Add ALL relevant offers to your card (there's no cost to add)
3. Shop at those merchants to automatically receive credits

**Typical offers:** $50 back on $200+ at Hilton, 5,000 bonus MR at specific retailers, percentage back at restaurants

**This alone can be worth $200-500/year in savings.**
